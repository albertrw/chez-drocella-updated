Welcome

Chez Drocella Eglantine Lodge – Your Eco-Friendly Retreat in Nyanza

Welcome to Chez Drocella Eglantine Lodge, a peaceful eco-lodge in the heart of Rwanda’s Southern Province. Located directly opposite the scenic Nyamagana Lake, our lodge is surrounded by native trees, offering an immersive nature experience and panoramic views of Nyanza’s lush hills.

Just 5 minutes from Nyanza Town, our lodge is perfect for travelers seeking authentic Rwandan hospitality, nature lovers, and cultural explorers. Our beautifully designed rooms feature traditional textiles and local artwork, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. Join us for nights of Rwandan storytelling, music, and dance under the stars, or explore nearby hiking and biking trails with spectacular lake views.

Our Background

Comfort, Culture & Community in the Heart of Rwanda

Chez Drocella Eglantine Lodge is more than a peaceful stay — it's a place with purpose. Originally a safe space for women affected by HIV/AIDS and an orphanage for children impacted by the Rwandan genocide, the lodge now supports local development by donating a portion of profits to women’s empowerment and child education projects.

Guests experience not only the comfort of a modern lodge but also authentic Rwandan cultural experiences such as basket weaving, bird watching, African cuisine tasting, and traditional music. Led by the passionate Madam Drocella, our lodge is a gateway to Rwanda’s soul — preserving heritage, promoting eco-tourism, and connecting guests with the local community.
